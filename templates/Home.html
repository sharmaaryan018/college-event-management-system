Event Rules & Guidelines

Code of Conduct

All participants must adhere to the event code of conduct, maintaining professionalism and respect throughout the event.

Registration

Pre-registration is mandatory for all events. Please ensure that you register before the event's deadline to secure your spot.

Event Timing

All events will start and end as per the scheduled time. Participants are advised to be punctual and respect the time slots.

Cancellation Policy

If you need to cancel your participation, please notify the event organizers at least 24 hours before the event starts to allow reallocation of your spot.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for an event?

You can register for any event by visiting the event listing page and clicking on the "Register" button for the respective event. Make sure you're logged in to your account.

Can I cancel my registration?

Yes, you can cancel your registration by going to your dashboard, selecting the event, and choosing the cancel option.

What if I miss an event?

If you miss an event, there might be recordings or materials available later, depending on the event. Please contact the event organizer for more information.
